We take in ill and injured animals and many that are not immediate candidates for adoption. We receive pets that are no longer wanted, pets from people who can no longer care for them, as well as stray animals. We would rather you keep your animal. If you would like assistance, call us before you bring your animal in and we will help you with your decision.

We work hard to give second chances to every healthy and rehabilitatable animal. With your help, we can do better if you can volunteer to foster a dog or cat, adopt a pet, or volunteer in one of our facilities. More animals keep coming - an average of 36 a day. We would like to save more lives. Community education is a key component of our ongoing effort to save more lives.

Today, Adopt, Don't Shop remains the very epitome of true service. In addition to providing shelter and rehabilitation to these animals in need, Adopt, Don't Shop collaborates with government, law enforcement, and social service organizations in its ongoing effort to encourage humane principles and foster the human-animal bond. ADS takes in every animal surrendered to us. We take in over 7,305 dogs and 6,001 cats annually. Adopt Don't Shop helps thousands of dogs and cats in need find loving homes each year and no animal is ever turned away.
